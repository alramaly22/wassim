More About Us

Coach Wasim Hassan is a renowned professional champion in the NBC federation with an impressive track record of success. His journey to excellence began with earning his pro card at Mr. Olympia Lebanon, followed by victories at Mr. World and the Dubai International, where he was crowned Champion of Champions. With a pro card from ELITE, Coach Wasim is a leading figure in his field. Not only has he excelled as an athlete, but he also shines as a dedicated trainer. Based in the UAE, he offers tailored training and nutritional programs designed to transform bodies and lives. His personalized approach helps clients achieve remarkable results, whether it's building muscle, losing weight, or preparing for competitions.

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I see results?

You can start seeing noticeable changes in your body within a few weeks. With my customized training and nutrition plans, many clients achieve significant transformations in a short period.

Can I really transform my body?

Absolutely! I've helped numerous clients completely transform their bodies and lives. Whether you want to build muscle, lose weight, or prepare for a competition, my programs are designed to deliver results.

What makes your programs different?

My programs are personalized to fit your specific goals and lifestyle. I provide daily support and guidance to ensure you're on track, making it easier to achieve your desired body transformation.

How do you support clients during their transformation?

I offer daily WhatsApp follow-ups to provide constant motivation and adjust your plan as needed. This hands-on approach helps ensure you're making progress and staying motivated throughout your journey.

Is your program suitable for everyone?

Yes, my programs are tailored to fit individual needs, whether you're a beginner or an experienced athlete. I work closely with each client to ensure they achieve their body transformation goals safely and effectively.
